Sangonomiya Kokomi(Japanese: 珊さん瑚ご宮のみや心ここ海み ) is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact. The young Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island and a descendant of the Sangonomiya Clan, Kokomi is in charge of most of Watatsumi's affairs, shouldering heavy responsibilities alone in hopes for giving Watatsumi Island's people the hopes and happiness that they desire.
